Key Insight: Studies show gamification increases engagement when there are elements of customization & storytelling, achievement & progression, and community connectedness.

User Preferences on Team Dynamics & Motivation

Members prefer working individually — When given a choice, members prefer working independently to maintain autonomy and avoid social loafing.

If in teams, familiar people preferred — Members prefer teaming up with friends, family, or colleagues they know rather than random groups.

Achievements/badges universally motivating — In both company-wide and Headspace-led challenges, members motivated by badges and charitable donations.

Competition was polarizing — Some loved competing for habit-building; others disliked gamification of mindfulness.

Additional Insight: Members feel comfortable discussing meditation with friends, family, or coworkers, suggesting community discussion could add value.
