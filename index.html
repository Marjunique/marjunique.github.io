About

Marjunique Louis is a design researcher specializing in accessibility and language access. Her work focuses on understanding how people interact with services and systems, particularly when language, accessibility, or policy creates barriers.

She works with government agencies, nonprofits, and educational institutions to make services more equitable and accessible. Her research has directly influenced policy changes, service improvements, and platform redesigns serving thousands of users.
